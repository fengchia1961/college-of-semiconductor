學校簡介A short introduction to your university （200 words ）
逢甲大學緊鄰中科、精密機械園區及快速發展的水湳經貿園區，匯集台積電、台灣美光等科技大廠。本校具備豐沛產官研資源，產學合作經費突破6億，穩居全臺綜合私校第一。
為精準對接產業需求，逢甲攜手台積電、美光、旺宏等企業開設冠名學程，其中申請修習「台積電半導體學程」人數，居全臺之首。116學年度更將成立涵蓋全產業鏈的「半導體學院」，結合志聖等企業與水湳校區的創新動能，穩健培育具備高度實戰力的半導體尖端人才。
Feng Chia University is strategically located adjacent to the Central Taiwan Science Park, the Precision Machinery Innovation Technology Park, and the rapidly developing Shuinan Economic and Trade Park, forming a hub for tech giants such as TSMC and Micron. Boasting abundant resources across industry, government, and research sectors, the university's industry-academia collaboration funding has surpassed NT$600 million, firmly securing its position as the top comprehensive private university in Taiwan.
To precisely align with industry demands, Feng Chia has partnered with leading enterprises including TSMC, Micron, and Macronix to offer corporate-sponsored academic programs. Notably, the number of students applying for the "TSMC Semiconductor Certificate Program" is the highest nationwide. Furthermore, in the 2027 academic year, the university is set to establish a "College of Semiconductor" encompassing the entire industry chain. By integrating the innovative momentum of the Shuinan Campus with enterprises such as C Sun, the university is committed to steadily cultivating cutting-edge semiconductor professionals equipped with highly practical skills.

短期研習課程內容簡述Brief Overview of the Short-Term Program 
(中文200字，英文300 字 ，含課程期間及合作廠商)
本課程以美國高中生或大學生為對象，設計半導體入門、臺灣半導體產業概況、後段製程、量測與測試基礎等主題，搭配校內實驗室參訪、基礎實作、企業參訪及專業華語課程。課程將協助學生理解半導體產業鏈、臺灣產業優勢與實務應用情境，並透過文化體驗與跨國交流提升其對臺灣科技教育與產業環境之認識。合作廠商可視後續洽談結果納入欣銓科技、矽品精密工業、台灣美光、旺宏電子、志聖工業等半導體相關企業。
Targeting high school and university students from the U.S., this course features core topics such as an introduction to semiconductors, an overview of Taiwan's semiconductor industry, back-end processing, and the fundamentals of metrology and testing. These academic modules are complemented by on-campus laboratory tours, basic hands-on training, corporate visits, and Mandarin for Specific Purposes (MSP) courses.
The program is designed to help students understand the semiconductor supply chain, Taiwan's industrial advantages, and practical application scenarios. Furthermore, through cultural experiences and cross-cultural exchanges, it aims to enhance their understanding of Taiwan's tech education and industrial landscape. Subject to further negotiations, potential corporate partners for the program may include semiconductor-related enterprises such as Ardentec (欣銓), SPIL (矽品), Micron (台灣美光), Macronix (旺宏), and C SUN (志聖).

Corporate Internship Plan: In the second semester, students will be matched—based on their specific expertise—with companies such as Micron Technology, SPIL, Ardentec, Macronix, and C SUN for internships and corporate visits.

方案內容簡述 Overview of the prorgam (中文200字，英文300字 ，含課程期間及合作廠商)
美國高中生半導體體驗與臺灣科技文化交流方案／Semiconductor Experience and Taiwan Technology-Culture Program for U.S. High School Students
本方案可結合臺中地區具國際交流經驗之高中夥伴，於暑假期間（7~8月）接待美國高中生短期來臺，交流期間1~2週，安排半導體導論、科技產業參訪（中部、新竹科學園區及欣銓、矽品、美光、旺宏、志聖等半導體相關企業）、校園實驗室體驗、華語與文化課程。逢甲大學可作為高等教育端之課程與實作支援平台，協助美國高中生提早接觸臺灣半導體產業與工程教育環境，並作為未來大學端交流或招生合作之基礎。
This initiative can be conducted in collaboration with partner high schools in the Taichung area that possess experience in international exchanges, to host American high school students for short-term visits to Taiwan during the summer break (July to August). The 1-to-2-week exchange program will feature an introduction to semiconductors, technology industry visits (including the Central Taiwan Science Park and Hsinchu Science Park, as well as semiconductor-related enterprises such as Ardentec, SPIL, Micron, Macronix, and C SUN), on-campus laboratory experiences, alongside Mandarin language and cultural courses.
Feng Chia University will serve as the higher education support platform for curriculum and practical training. This will assist American high school students in gaining early exposure to Taiwan's semiconductor industry and engineering education environment, thereby laying the foundation for future university-level academic exchanges and student recruitment collaborations.

貴校現行半導體教育國際合作亮點案例Best Practices in Semiconductor Education Cooperation（中文300字，英文400字 ）
本校積極布局全球半導體人才培育鏈，打造具國際競爭力的跨國雙聯學位與交流體系：
串聯美澳頂尖名校：攜手美國普渡、聖荷西州立及辛辛那提大學，結合學術研發、矽谷就業與Co-op實務，構築「學術、實務、就業」黃金三角；另結盟澳洲昆士蘭大學，深耕量子與半導體材料前瞻研究。
彈性學制精準對接：透過 2+2、3+1+1 等彈性雙學位學制，結合全英授課與跨國實習，無縫銜接海外高階課程，直擊全球供應鏈需求。
臺美雙向人才培訓：憑藉豐富的國際與產學經驗，採取「短期培訓先行、產學實作深化、長期學制銜接」策略。結合專業華語教學、封測後段製程實作與企業參訪，助外籍生掌握臺灣產業優勢，培育具國際移動力之尖端人才。
Our university is actively expanding its global semiconductor talent cultivation network through competitive transnational dual-degree and exchange programs:
Strategic Alliances with Top Global Universities
We have partnered with leading U.S. institutions—Purdue University, San Jose State University, and the University of Cincinnati—integrating academic R&amp;D, Silicon Valley’s job market, and advanced Co-op training to form a "golden triangle" of academia, practice, and employment. Additionally, our alliance with the University of Queensland in Australia extends our reach into cutting-edge quantum and semiconductor materials research.
Flexible Degrees &amp; Industry Alignment
Through flexible 2+2 and 3+1+1 dual-degree frameworks, students seamlessly transition to advanced overseas studies. This model integrates English-taught courses, mutual credit recognition, and transnational internships to precisely meet global supply chain demands.
Bilateral Taiwan-U.S. Talent Cultivation
Leveraging our robust experience in international collaboration and Mandarin teaching, we adopt a phased strategy: prioritizing short-term training, deepening hands-on industry practices, and bridging into long-term degrees. By focusing on back-end processing, metrology, and packaging, we provide corporate visits and lab experiences to help international students understand Taiwan’s semiconductor strengths while cultivating practical talent with global mobility.
